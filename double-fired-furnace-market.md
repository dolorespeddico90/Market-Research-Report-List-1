<p><p>Double fired furnaces operate in two primary market types: atmosphere and vacuum. Atmosphere furnaces utilize controlled atmospheric conditions to facilitate various heat treatment processes, enabling the processing of materials like metals and ceramics in a specific gas environment to prevent oxidation. In contrast, vacuum furnaces operate under reduced pressure, eliminating atmospheric contaminants and allowing for high-temperature treatments without oxidation or contamination. Both types are essential in industries requiring precise thermal processing for advanced material properties.</p></p>
