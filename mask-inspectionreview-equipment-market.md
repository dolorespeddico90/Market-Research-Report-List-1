<p><ul><li>Die to Die (DD) Method</li><li>Die to Database (DB) Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mask Inspection/Review Equipment market features two primary methods: Die to Die (DD) and Die to Database (DB). The DD method inspects individual semiconductor dies to identify defects by comparing one die against another, ensuring consistency and quality in production. Conversely, the DB method involves comparing each die against a reference database of known good dies, allowing for comprehensive defect characterization and process improvement. Both methods play critical roles in enhancing yield and reliability in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
