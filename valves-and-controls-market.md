<p><p>The valves and controls market encompasses various types, each serving specific applications. Sliding-stem globe valves provide precise flow control with a vertically rising stem. V-notch ball valves offer swift regulation in fluid flow, ideal for throttling. Butterfly valves are compact and lightweight, suitable for on/off and throttling services in larger pipelines. Angle valves facilitate flow redirection in confined spaces. Other types include check valves and pinch valves, addressing diverse industrial needs across sectors like oil, gas, water treatment, and more.</p></p>
