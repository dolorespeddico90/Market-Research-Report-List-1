<p><p>The automotive parking system market comprises three main types: mechanical systems, semi-automated systems, and automated systems. Mechanical systems involve fixed structures that use lifts or platforms for vehicle storage, providing space efficiency. Semi-automated systems require some human intervention, allowing operators to assist in parking while still optimizing space. Automated systems fully mechanize the parking process, using advanced technology and robotics to park and retrieve vehicles without human involvement, enhancing efficiency and convenience in urban environments.</p></p>
