<p><p>Narrowband IoT (NB-IoT) chips primarily support two market segments: LTE Category M and NB-IoT technology itself. LTE Category M, or LTE-M, provides a balance of low power consumption and moderate data rates, suitable for applications needing mobility, like connected vehicles. In contrast, NB-IoT is optimized for fixed low-bandwidth applications, offering extensive coverage and longer battery life, making it ideal for smart meters and environmental monitoring. Both segments enhance connectivity for the growing IoT ecosystem.</p></p>
