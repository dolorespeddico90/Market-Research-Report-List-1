<p><p>The plywood market consists of various types catering to different applications. Softwood plywood is sourced from coniferous trees, ideal for construction and structural uses. Aircraft plywood is engineered for aviation, emphasizing strength and weight. Decorative plywood features overlaid surfaces for visual appeal. Flexible plywood is used in bending applications, while marine plywood is treated for water resistance, suitable for boats and aquatic environments. Other types encompass niche applications.</p></p>
