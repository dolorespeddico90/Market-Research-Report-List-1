<p><p>The wood pellets fuel market primarily comprises two types: white pellets and black pellets. White pellets are made from clean, virgin wood materials, characterized by their high energy efficiency and lower moisture content. They are commonly used for residential heating and industrial applications. In contrast, black pellets are produced from densified biomass that may include sawmill residuals and agricultural waste, offering a higher energy density and suitable for transportation and storage. Both types cater to the growing demand for renewable energy sources.</p></p>
