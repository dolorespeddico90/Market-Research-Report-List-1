<p><p>The plastic pipe fittings market comprises various materials, each with unique properties and applications. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) is widely used due to its versatility and cost-effectiveness. ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene) is recognized for its strength and durability, commonly used in drain, waste, and vent systems. PE (Polyethylene) is flexible, resistant to chemicals, and suitable for outdoor applications. Nylon, known for its toughness, is often used in specialty fittings and high-pressure systems.</p></p>
