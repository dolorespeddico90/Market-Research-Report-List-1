<p><p>LED industrial lighting encompasses various types, primarily high/bay and area lighting. High bay lighting is designed for spaces with high ceilings, typically used in warehouses and large manufacturing facilities, offering powerful illumination to enhance productivity. In contrast, low bay lighting suits areas with lower ceilings, providing efficient lighting for spaces like retail stores or workshops. Area lighting focuses on illuminating outdoor spaces such as parking lots and pathways, ensuring safety and visibility. Both types promote energy efficiency and longer lifespan compared to traditional lighting.</p></p>
