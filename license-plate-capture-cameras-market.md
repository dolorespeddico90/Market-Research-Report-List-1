<p><p>License Plate Capture Cameras (LPR) are classified into three main types: mobile, fixed, and portable. Mobile LPR cameras are integrated into vehicles, enabling real-time monitoring on the move, ideal for law enforcement and patrols. Fixed LPR cameras are stationary installations that capture vehicle plates at specific locations, often used for traffic management and security. Portable LPR cameras are versatile units that can be easily moved, allowing for temporary setups in various locations, useful for events or parking management.</p></p>
