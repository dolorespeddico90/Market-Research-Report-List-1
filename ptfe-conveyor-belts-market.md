<p><p>The PTFE conveyor belts market features two primary types: PTFE seamless conveyor belts and PTFE mesh conveyor belts. PTFE seamless conveyor belts are characterized by their smooth surface, offering excellent chemical resistance and non-stick properties, making them ideal for food processing and industrial applications. PTFE mesh conveyor belts, on the other hand, have an open structure that facilitates airflow and drainage, making them suitable for drying and cooling processes. Both types provide durability and performance in demanding environments, enhancing operational efficiency.</p></p>
