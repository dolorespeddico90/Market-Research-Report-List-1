<p><p>The electronic display market encompasses various technologies, including LCD, LED, and OLED. LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) uses liquid crystals and a backlight to produce images, offering good color accuracy but limited viewing angles. LED (Light Emitting Diode) displays enhance LCD technology by utilizing LEDs for backlighting, resulting in brighter images and improved energy efficiency. OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diode) offers superior contrast and color reproduction by allowing each pixel to emit its own light, providing deeper blacks and more vibrant colors.</p></p>
