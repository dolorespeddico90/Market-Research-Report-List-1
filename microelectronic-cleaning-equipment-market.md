<p><strong>The Microelectronic Cleaning Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Wafer Cryogenic Systems</li><li>Single-Wafer Spray Systems</li><li>Batch Immersion Cleaning Systems</li><li>Batch Spray Cleaning Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The microelectronic cleaning equipment market comprises several types designed for specific cleaning needs. Single-wafer cryogenic systems utilize ultra-cold gases to remove contaminants on individual wafers, ensuring precision cleaning. Single-wafer spray systems apply targeted sprays to clean individual wafers effectively. Batch immersion cleaning systems submerge multiple wafers in cleaning solutions for thorough treatment, while batch spray cleaning systems utilize sprays to cleanse multiple wafers simultaneously. Each system is tailored to enhance device performance and yield in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
