<p><p>The Air Horn Systems market comprises two main types: Pneumatic and Electropneumatic Air Horn Systems. Pneumatic Air Horn Systems utilize compressed air to produce sound, relying on mechanical components for operation. These systems are generally simpler and more durable, ideal for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, Electropneumatic Air Horn Systems combine electrical components with pneumatic mechanisms, allowing for more precise control and potentially louder outputs. This versatility makes them suitable for both commercial and industrial environments where varying sound levels are needed.</p></p>
