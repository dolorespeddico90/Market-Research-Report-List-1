<p><p>The echocardiogram machine market is categorized into two main types: cart/trolley-based and portable/handheld devices. Cart/trolley-based echocardiograms are larger, station-based systems typically used in hospital settings, offering advanced imaging capabilities and comprehensive diagnostics. In contrast, portable/handheld echocardiograms are compact and easy to transport, allowing for point-of-care applications in various settings, including clinics and home healthcare. The choice between these types depends on the clinical needs, ease of use, and required imaging sophistication.</p></p>
