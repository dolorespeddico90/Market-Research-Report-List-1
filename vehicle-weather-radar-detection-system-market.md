<p><p>The Vehicle Weather Radar Detection System market is categorized into three types: Low Frequency Radar, High Frequency Radar, and UHF Doppler Radar. Low Frequency Radar is used for long-range detection and can penetrate precipitation for improved accuracy. High Frequency Radar offers enhanced resolution for detecting small weather phenomena and is effective in short-range applications. UHF Doppler Radar specializes in analyzing the motion of precipitation, allowing for precise tracking of weather patterns and improving safety for vehicles in adverse conditions.</p></p>
