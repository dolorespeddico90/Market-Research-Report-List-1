<p><p>The Military Servo Drive market encompasses two main types: DC Servo Drives and AC Servo Drives. DC Servo Drives utilize direct current to control servo motors, offering precise speed and position control, ideal for applications requiring rapid response and fine-tuning. Conversely, AC Servo Drives operate on alternating current, providing high efficiency and better performance in demanding military environments. Both types support advanced motion control systems used in various military applications, including robotics, unmanned systems, and missile guidance.</p></p>
