<p><ul><li>General Purpose Polystyrene (GPPS)</li><li>High Impact Polystyrene (HIPS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Polystyrene (PS) resins market comprises primarily two types: General Purpose Polystyrene (GPPS) and High Impact Polystyrene (HIPS). GPPS is a clear, rigid plastic known for its excellent clarity and ease of processing, making it suitable for applications like packaging and disposable items. HIPS, on the other hand, is modified for enhanced toughness and impact resistance, making it ideal for products that require durability, such as appliances and toys. Both types cater to diverse industries including consumer goods, automotive, and electronics.</p></p>
