<p><p>Capacitive film vacuum gauges are precision instruments used to measure vacuum levels by detecting capacitance changes in thin films. These gauges operate based on the principle that the capacitance between two conductive plates varies with the atmospheric pressure acting on the film. They are widely utilized in various industries, including semiconductor manufacturing, metallurgy, and pharmaceuticals, where precise vacuum conditions are critical.</p><p>The Capacitive Film Vacuum Gauges Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.3% during the forecast period.
