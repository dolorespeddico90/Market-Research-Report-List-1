<p><p>A hydrobike is a water-based recreational vehicle that combines cycling and boating. It operates similarly to a bicycle but is designed to float on water, powered by pedaling that drives a propeller or paddle system. Hydrobikes are eco-friendly, easy to use, and popular for leisure activities, fitness, and tourism in lakes, rivers, and calm coastal waters. They are often made from lightweight, durable materials like aluminum or fiberglass, ensuring stability and buoyancy.</p><p>The hydrobike market is experiencing steady growth, driven by increasing interest in water-based recreational activities, eco-tourism, and fitness trends.
