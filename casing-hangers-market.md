<p><ul><li>Slip-type</li><li>Core Shaft Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Casing hangers are crucial components in the oil and gas industry, designed to support casing strings during drilling. The slip-type casing hangers utilize slips to grip the casing, ensuring stability and allowing for easy adjustments. In contrast, core shaft-type hangers feature a central shaft that holds the casing firmly, offering enhanced load-bearing capacity. Both types cater to different operational needs, with slip types being more adaptable for varied casing sizes, while core shaft types provide robust support in demanding conditions.</p></p>
