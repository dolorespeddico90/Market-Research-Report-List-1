<p><strong>The Well Testing Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Downhole Equipment</li><li>Surface Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The well testing equipment market is divided into two main categories: downhole equipment and surface equipment. Downhole equipment includes tools used within the wellbore, such as pressure gauges, flow meters, and sampling tools, which help assess reservoir performance. Surface equipment encompasses systems located on the surface, such as separators, piping, and data acquisition systems, that facilitate the collection, processing, and analysis of fluid samples. Together, these equipment types are essential for optimizing oil and gas well performance and ensuring safe operations.</p></p>
