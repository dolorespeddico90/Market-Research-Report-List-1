<p><p>The Connected Thermostats market is divided into two main types: Wired and Wireless Connected Thermostats. Wired Connected Thermostats are directly linked to a home's electrical system, providing stable connectivity and reliable control over climate management systems. They often require professional installation. In contrast, Wireless Connected Thermostats utilize Wi-Fi or Bluetooth technology, offering flexibility and ease of installation without the need for extensive wiring. These devices enable remote access and integration with smart home systems, enhancing user convenience and energy efficiency.</p></p>
