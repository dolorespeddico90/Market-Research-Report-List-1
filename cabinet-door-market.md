<p><p>The cabinet door market comprises three primary types: solid wood, wood mould pressing, and other materials. Solid wood doors are prized for their durability and natural beauty, often featuring intricate designs and finishes. Wood mould pressing involves layering wood fibers or veneers under high pressure to create strong, lightweight doors with consistent textures. Other market types may include composite materials, MDF, and laminate finishes, offering a range of aesthetics and cost-effectiveness suitable for diverse consumer preferences in home design.</p></p>
