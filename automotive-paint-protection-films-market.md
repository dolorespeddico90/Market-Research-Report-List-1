<p><p>The automotive paint protection films market comprises several types, each offering unique benefits. PVC type paint protection films are cost-effective but less durable, making them suitable for temporary protection. PU type films provide enhanced clarity and flexibility, offering better resistance to scratches and yellowing. TPU type films are known for their superior durability, self-healing properties, and UV resistance, making them a popular choice for long-term protection. Other films may include hybrid materials or specialty coatings tailored to specific automotive needs.</p></p>
