<p><p>The GaAs solar cell epitaxial wafer market can be categorized into three types: single junction, double junction, and triple junction. Single junction wafers utilize one layer of semiconductor material, offering basic efficiency. Double junction wafers enhance performance by integrating two layers, capturing a broader spectrum of sunlight. Triple junction wafers maximize efficiency further by employing three layers, optimizing energy conversion across a wider range of wavelengths. Each type serves varying applications, with triple junction wafers often used in space and high-performance settings.</p></p>
