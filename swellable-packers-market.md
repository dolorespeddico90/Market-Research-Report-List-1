<p><ul><li>Permanent Packers</li><li>Retrievable Packers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The swellable packers market comprises two primary types: permanent packers and retrievable packers. Permanent packers are designed for long-term applications and remain in place once set, providing a reliable seal within the wellbore, often used in enhanced oil recovery. In contrast, retrievable packers can be removed after use, allowing for flexibility in well operations, maintenance, or changes in production strategy. Both types serve distinct purposes in optimizing oil and gas extraction processes while ensuring well integrity.</p></p>
