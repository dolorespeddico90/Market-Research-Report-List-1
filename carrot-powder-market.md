<p><strong>The Carrot Powder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spray Drying Carrot Powder</li><li>Freeze-dried Carrot Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carrot powder market primarily consists of two types: spray-dried carrot powder and freeze-dried carrot powder. Spray-drying involves rapidly evaporating moisture from carrot puree, resulting in a fine powder that retains essential nutrients and has a long shelf life. It is commonly used in food products for convenience. In contrast, freeze-dried carrot powder undergoes a process where moisture is removed at low temperatures, preserving flavor and color. This type is often preferred for high-quality applications, such as gourmet foods and supplements.</p></p>
