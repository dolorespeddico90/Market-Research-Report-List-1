<p><ul><li>Voltage Type Frequency Conversion Controller</li><li>Current Type Frequency Conversion Controller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The frequency conversion controller market is divided into two main types: voltage type and current type controllers. Voltage type frequency conversion controllers regulate output frequency by varying the voltage supplied to the load, making them suitable for applications where voltage stability is critical. In contrast, current type frequency conversion controllers manage output frequency through the control of current, offering advantages in efficiency and load adaptability. Both types serve various industries, including manufacturing and power generation, optimizing performance in variable frequency applications.</p></p>
