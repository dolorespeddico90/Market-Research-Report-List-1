<p><p>The Autonomous Light Vehicle market is divided into Fully-autonomous and Semi-autonomous segments. Fully-autonomous vehicles operate without human intervention, utilizing advanced AI, sensors, and mapping systems for complete self-driving capabilities. They are designed for Level 4 and Level 5 autonomy, handling all driving tasks in specific or all environments. Semi-autonomous vehicles, on the other hand, require human oversight and assist with driving tasks like lane-keeping, adaptive cruise control, or parking, typically corresponding to Level 2 or Level 3 autonomy. Both aim to enhance safety, efficiency, and convenience in transportation.</p></p>
