<p><p>The polypropylene compression fittings market is categorized into several types based on their design and function. Elbow type fittings enable directional changes, essential for navigating around obstacles in piping systems. Reducer type fittings assist in connecting pipes of different diameters, ensuring smooth transitions and flow efficiency. Other types may include caps, plugs, and adapters, catering to various application needs across industries such as plumbing and HVAC.</p></p>
