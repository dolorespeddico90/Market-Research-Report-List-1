<p><p>Crawler combines are essential agricultural machines designed for harvesting crops in various terrains. There are two primary market types: wheeled and crawler. Wheeled combines are suited for firm, flat ground, offering speed and maneuverability, making them ideal for large, open fields. In contrast, crawler combines have tracks that provide better traction and stability on uneven or muddy terrain, reducing soil compaction. Each type serves specific agricultural needs, catering to different soil conditions and farming practices.</p></p>
