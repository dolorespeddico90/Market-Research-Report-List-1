<p><strong>The Cooling Blower Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Brushed</li><li>Brushless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cooling blower market is primarily divided into two types: brushed and brushless. Brushed blowers utilize a commutator and brushes, providing a simple, cost-effective design suitable for low-power applications. However, they tend to have a shorter lifespan and require more maintenance due to friction wear. Brushless blowers, on the other hand, employ electronic commutation, resulting in higher efficiency, longer operational life, and reduced noise. While typically more expensive, brushless blowers are favored in demanding applications requiring reliability and performance.</p></p>
