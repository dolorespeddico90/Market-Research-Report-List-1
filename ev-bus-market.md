<p><p>The EV bus market includes Battery Electric Buses (BEBs) and Hybrid Buses. BEBs are fully electric, relying solely on rechargeable batteries, offering zero emissions and lower operating costs. They are ideal for urban transit with fixed routes and charging infrastructure. Hybrid Buses combine internal combustion engines with electric propulsion, reducing fuel consumption and emissions. They suit areas with limited charging infrastructure, providing flexibility and extended range. Both types aim to reduce environmental impact, with BEBs focusing on sustainability and Hybrid Buses balancing efficiency and practicality.</p></p>
