<p><ul><li>Accelerometer</li><li>Gyroscope</li><li>Magnetometer</li><li>Combo Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The embedded sensor market encompasses various sensor types, each serving distinct purposes. Accelerometers measure acceleration forces, enabling motion detection and orientation sensing. Gyroscopes provide rotational data, crucial for navigation and stabilization. Magnetometers detect magnetic fields, often used for compass functionalities and location tracking. Combo sensors integrate multiple functionalities, combining accelerometer, gyroscope, and magnetometer capabilities in a single device, enhancing performance and reducing space requirements. Together, these sensors play integral roles in applications like smartphones, wearable devices, and automotive systems.</p></p>
