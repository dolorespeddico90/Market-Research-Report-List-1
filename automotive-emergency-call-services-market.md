<p><p>Automotive Emergency Call Services, also known as eCall or emergency call systems, are in-vehicle safety features designed to automatically or manually contact emergency services in the event of an accident or critical situation. They are increasingly integrated into modern vehicles as part of advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS) and connected car technologies.</p><p>The Automotive Emergency Call Services Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.2% during the forecast period. As automotive safety becomes a priority, the demand for emergency call services is set to rise, making it a critical component of future mobility solutions.</p></p>
