<p><strong>The Carbon Fiber Reinforced Thermoplastic Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polyacrylonitrile (PAN)-based CFRTP</li><li>Pitch-based CFRTP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Carbon Fiber Reinforced Thermoplastic (CFRTP) market is primarily categorized into two types: Polyacrylonitrile (PAN)-based CFRTP and Pitch-based CFRTP. PAN-based CFRTP utilizes polyacrylonitrile as a precursor, offering high tensile strength and thermal stability, making it suitable for aerospace and automotive applications. In contrast, Pitch-based CFRTP is derived from petroleum pitch, providing excellent electrical conductivity and lower density, which is advantageous in electrical and sporting goods industries. Each type serves distinct applications based on their unique properties and performance characteristics.</p></p>
