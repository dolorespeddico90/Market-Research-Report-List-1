<p><p>The Automotive Software Over The Air (OTA) market consists of two primary types: Cellular and Wi-Fi. Cellular OTA utilizes mobile networks, allowing vehicles to receive software updates and data remotely while on the move, ensuring continuous connectivity and convenience. On the other hand, Wi-Fi OTA allows updates when the vehicle is parked and connected to a local Wi-Fi network, offering a cost-effective solution that minimizes data usage. Both types are essential for enhancing vehicle functionality and security.</p></p>
