<p><p>The biometric sensors market encompasses various types of technologies used for identification and authentication. Capacitive sensors detect fingerprints by measuring changes in electrical charge. Optical sensors capture detailed images of finger patterns or irises. Thermal sensors identify users based on heat patterns emitted by their skin. Ultrasound sensors utilize sound waves to map facial features or fingerprints. Electric field sensors measure variations in the electric field caused by the presence of a finger, enhancing security in biometric systems.</p></p>
