<p><p>The antiblock masterbatch market is categorized into two main types: organic and inorganic. Organic antiblock masterbatches typically consist of natural substances that provide slip and anti-blocking properties, enhancing the performance of plastic films. In contrast, inorganic antiblock masterbatches utilize mineral-based additives, such as silica or talc, which create a physical barrier between layers of film, reducing adhesion. Both types are essential for improving the clarity, gloss, and handling characteristics of packaging materials, catering to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
