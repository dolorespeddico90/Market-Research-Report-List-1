<p><p>The NSAID market comprises various drug classes that relieve pain and reduce inflammation. Salicylates, like aspirin, inhibit platelet aggregation and provide cardiovascular benefits. Propionic acid derivatives, such as ibuprofen, offer versatile pain relief. Acetic acid derivatives, including diclofenac, are potent anti-inflammatory agents. Enolic acid derivatives (oxycams) feature drugs like piroxicam, known for long-lasting effects. Anthranilic acid derivatives (fenamates), such as mefenamic acid, serve as alternatives for mild to moderate pain. Selective COX-2 inhibitors (coxibs), like celecoxib, target inflammation with fewer gastrointestinal side effects, while sulfonanilides include drugs like nimesulide, offering additional anti-inflammatory benefits.</p></p>
