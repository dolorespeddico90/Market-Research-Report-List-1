<p><p>The diaphragm pump market encompasses several types, including Air Operated Diaphragm Pumps (AODD), Electric Driven Diaphragm Pumps (EODD), and Piston Diaphragm Pumps. AODDs utilize compressed air to operate, making them suitable for hazardous environments. EODDs are powered by electric motors, offering efficiency and precision, ideal for varied applications. Piston Diaphragm Pumps combine piston and diaphragm mechanisms, delivering high-pressure capabilities for demanding tasks. Each type serves specific industry needs based on fluid compatibility, pressure requirements, and operational environment.</p></p>
