<p><strong>The Photoresist Stripper Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Type Photoresist Stripper</li><li>Negative Type Photoresist Stripper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photoresist stripper market is primarily divided into two types: positive and negative photoresist strippers. Positive type photoresist strippers are designed to remove photoresist layers that have been exposed to light, allowing the unexposed areas to remain on the substrate. In contrast, negative type photoresist strippers target photoresist that hardens upon exposure, effectively dissolving the unexposed areas. These products are essential in semiconductor manufacturing and microfabrication processes, enabling precise pattern transfer and ensuring high-quality surface treatment.</p></p>
