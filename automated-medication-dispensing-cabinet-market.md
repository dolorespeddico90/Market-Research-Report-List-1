<p><p>The Automated Medication Dispensing Cabinet (AMDC) market features two main types: centralized and decentralized dispensing cabinets. Centralized dispensing cabinets are typically located in pharmacy areas, allowing for bulk storage and controlled access to medications for multiple departments, enhancing efficiency and inventory management. Decentralized dispensing cabinets, on the other hand, are situated closer to patient care areas, providing immediate access to medications at the point of care, improving workflow and enabling quicker response times for patient needs.</p></p>
