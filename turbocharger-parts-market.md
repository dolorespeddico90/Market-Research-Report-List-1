<p><p>The turbocharger parts market can be segmented into several key types: </p><p>1. **Compressor Wheels** boost air intake, enhancing engine performance.</p><p>2. **Turbine Wheels** harness exhaust gases to drive the compressor.</p><p>3. **Compressor Housings** encase the compressor wheel, directing air flow.</p><p>4. **Turbine Housings** channel exhaust gases towards the turbine wheel. </p><p>5. **Center Housings** support both the turbine and compressor, allowing for balanced operation. </p><p>6. **Others** category includes various components like actuators and wastegates essential for turbocharger functionality.</p></p>
