<p><p>Pour Point Depressants (PPDs) are chemical additives used in crude oil to lower its pour point, the temperature at which the oil ceases to flow. They work by modifying the wax crystals formed in crude oil at low temperatures, preventing them from interlocking and solidifying. This ensures smoother transportation and handling of crude oil, especially in cold climates or during winter months. PPDs are essential in the oil and gas industry to maintain operational efficiency and reduce costs associated with pipeline blockages and equipment failures.</p><p>The Pour Point Depressant for Crude Oil Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.9% during the forecast period.
