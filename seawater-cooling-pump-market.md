<p><strong>The Seawater Cooling Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Pumps</li><li>Positive Displacement Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The seawater cooling pump market primarily includes two types: centrifugal pumps and positive displacement pumps. Centrifugal pumps utilize rotational energy to move seawater efficiently, making them ideal for large-scale cooling applications, such as in power plants and industrial facilities. In contrast, positive displacement pumps operate by trapping a fixed volume of seawater and forcing it through the system, providing consistent flow rates, which is beneficial for specific applications requiring higher pressure and precision. Both types cater to diverse cooling needs in various industries.</p></p>
