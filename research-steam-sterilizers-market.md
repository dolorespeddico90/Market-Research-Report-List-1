<p><strong>The Research Steam Sterilizers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravity Cycle</li><li>SFPP Cycle</li><li>Pre-Vac Cycle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Research steam sterilizers are categorized into three main types based on their sterilization cycles: </p><p>1. **Gravity Cycle**: This method relies on gravity to displace air from the chamber, allowing steam to penetrate the load effectively. It's suitable for items that can withstand higher temperatures.</p><p>2. **SFPP Cycle (Steam Flush Pressure Pulse)**: This cycle uses a combination of steam flushes and pressure pulses to enhance steam penetration, ideal for porous loads.</p><p>3. **Pre-Vac Cycle**: This method involves removing air beforehand, creating a vacuum to facilitate rapid steam penetration, making it efficient for delicate instruments. Each type serves specific sterilization needs in research settings.</p></p>
