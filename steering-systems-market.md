<p><ul><li>Electronic Power Steering (EPS)</li><li>Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS)</li><li>Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steering systems market consists of three main types: Electronic Power Steering (EPS), Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS), and Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS). EPS utilizes electric motors to assist steering, enhancing energy efficiency and offering precise control. EHPS combines electric and hydraulic systems, providing a balance of performance and efficiency. HPS relies solely on hydraulic pressure generated by a pump, delivering consistent response but with greater energy consumption. Each system caters to different vehicle types and consumer demands.</p></p>
