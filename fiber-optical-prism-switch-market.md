<p><strong>The Fiber Optical Prism Switch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Mode Fiber Switch</li><li>Multimode Fiber Switch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fiber Optical Prism Switch market is categorized into two main types: Single Mode Fiber Switch and Multimode Fiber Switch. Single Mode Fiber Switches use a small core diameter, allowing only one light mode to propagate, which provides higher bandwidth and longer distances, making them ideal for telecommunications. In contrast, Multimode Fiber Switches have a larger core, enabling multiple light modes to transmit simultaneously, suitable for shorter distances and typically used in data centers and local area networks.</p></p>
